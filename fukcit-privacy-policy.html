Our Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26/06/2023


This Privacy Policy describes how Fukcit Creative ("the Company"), a design agency based in South Africa, collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website and utilize our services. By accessing or using our website and services, you acknowledge and accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.



Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:


  • Personal Information:
    When clients sign up for our website subscription options, we may collect their names, email addresses, and payment information for billing purposes.
  • Cookies:
    We may use cookies to collect information about your browsing activities and preferences. This helps us enhance your user experience and improve our website.
  • Geo-location Information:
    We may collect limited geo-location information from visitors to better understand the geographical distribution of our audience. However, no additional data will be collected from visitors unless they become subscription purchasers.


Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:


  • Providing Services:
    We use clients' personal information to give them access to our design services as part of their subscription.
  • Business Partners:
    With the clients' consent, we may share their information with our trusted business partners to facilitate referrals and collaborations.


User Rights


  • Consent:
    Sharing of personal information with our business partners will only occur if clients explicitly consent to these terms. Clients have the right to deny permission for sharing and can request their data to be removed after services are canceled or denied.
  • Data Access and Modification:
    Clients have the right to access, update, and correct their personal information by contacting us at the email address provided below.

Security Measures


We take reasonable measures to protect the confidentiality and security of your information. Client data is stored on secured Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platforms such as Trello or Notion, and payment information is processed securely through PayPal.


Cookies


We use cookies to enhance user experience and gather analytics. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in our Cookie Policy.


Applicable Laws and Jurisdiction


This Privacy Policy is governed by and shall be construed in accordance with the laws of South Africa. Any disputes arising from this Privacy Policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in South Africa.


Updates to the Privacy Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website.
